About PolyCode

learn • code • execute

Our Mission

PolyCode is an educational coding platform by Edifica that helps students practice Java, C/C++, Python, and SQL with a clean, distraction-free interface. You can write code, provide live input, visualize output, and get quick complexity insights—all in the browser.

What Makes PolyCode Different

  • Zero-setup coding—just open and run.
  • Unified UI across multiple languages.
  • Practical, print-friendly references and examples.
  • Thoughtful design for classrooms and labs.

Roadmap

  • Deeper complexity tracing & visualization.
  • Interactive compilers for additional languages.
  • Beginner-friendly projects and auto-graded challenges.
