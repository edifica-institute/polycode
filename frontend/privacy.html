Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 29, 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how PolyCode (“we”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Service.

2. Data We Process

  • Usage Data: anonymized telemetry such as pages visited, features used, general device info.
  • Content Data: code you upload or type into the editor to compile/execute or analyze.
  • Contact Data: information you provide when you reach out (e.g., email, name).

3. How We Use Data

  • Provide and improve coding/analysis features.
  • Secure the platform, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • Respond to support requests and communicate important updates.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

We may use essential cookies or local storage to remember preferences (e.g., theme, last opened language). You can control cookies via your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use hosting, deployment, analytics, or error-tracking providers. These services may process limited data under their own privacy terms.

6. Data Retention

Compilation artifacts (e.g., temporary files) are generally ephemeral and cleared after sessions. Support communications may be retained as required to address issues.

7. Security

We employ reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data; however, no method is 100% secure.

8. Your Choices

  • Contact us to request deletion of support emails or inquire about data handling.
  • You may opt-out of non-essential cookies through browser controls.

9. Children’s Privacy

The Service is intended for users who can lawfully consent. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us for removal.

10. Changes

We may update this policy periodically. We will revise the “Last updated” date when changes occur.
